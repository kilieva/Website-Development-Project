    • Courchevel

    Ideally located on the Croisette, Clair de Lune Courchevel is the perfect place to indulge in a day out on the slopes and enjoy the festive atmosphere of Courchevel and its numerous boutiques, bars and restaurants.

    Courchevel, 70 Years of History

    On the occasion of the resort’s 70th anniversary, the “Patio de la Croisette” hosts a retrospective of the pioneer story of the village of Courchevel. Through a collection of 28 boards adorned with unique photographs and iconography, the first-ever chalets and the iconic resort personalities are revealed to curious travellers.

    Ski Service

    Every morning, the dedicated Ski Service team carefully prepares each guest’s equipment as well as little treats and thoughtful surprises to delight skiers.

    La Grande Dévallée

    Get ready, get set and snow! On Wednesday, April 5th, 2017, the legendary race of La Grande Dévallée showcases a series of unique activities that will delight the biggest thrill-seekers. From water slide races to gymkhana, the event invites the most audacious travellers to test their limits!

    Hublot Slalom

    On February 25th, 2018, Cheval Blanc Courchevel hosts the much anticipated Hublot Slalom in partnership with Courchevel 1850’s Ecole de Ski Français. An annual downhill rendezvous, this competition puts to the test speed, strength, and coordination.

    Courchevel Secret Places

    This season, an exhibition featuring five photographers unveils Courchevel as you have never seen it before. From winter dawn’s glow to graceful secluded places, discover these unseen photographs on the patio of the Croisette.

    Gem of the Alps

    For those who have already experienced the endless delights of Courchevel, its charm and appeal are relatively obvious. Boasting the most fantastic luxurious hotel in France, an extraordinary array of opportunities for adventure both on and off the slopes, it really is the kind of treasure that is genuinely one of a kind.
