    • Rooms & Suites

    Designed by renowned architect Pierre Wilmotte, Clair de Lune Courchevel offers twenty-six contemporary rooms. Each with mountain flair, some feature balconies or open terraces with beautiful views over the slopes or the legendary village. All have white-washed walls, vaulted ceilings, and hand-made tiles on the floors.

    Superior Room

    With a lovely view over the vibrant ski resort of Courchevel, the Superior Room offers a sporty winter escape at the foot of the ski slopes.

    Family Room

    With a panoramic view over the ski resort, the Family Room welcomes children and adults in an inviting and comfortable space. It includes two sun-filled bedrooms with their adjoining bathrooms.

    Privilege Room

    A cosy retreat for a stay with friends or family, the Privilege Room offers a sprawling view over the prestigious village of Courchevel.

    Exclusive Room

    With views opening onto either the Croisette or the village, the Exclusive Room offers a spacious and homely world to enjoy every aspect of a mountain getaway.

    Deluxe Room

    Offering spectacular views over the village, the Deluxe Room features a lovely living area with a sofa bed, for a winter escape with family or friends.

    Room features

    Style

    Our twenty-six rooms are decorated with contemporary furniture coming from Europe, following the eclectic style which is so characteristic of its charm. The bathrooms are made of hand-made tiles. A hair dryer is at your disposal in every bathroom.

    Features

    All rooms have a telephone, a flat screen plasma TV with all satellite channels and a DVD player, a safe, air conditioning, minibar and an I-Pod docking station. If you forgot to bring your own I-Pod, we'll gladly lend you one of ours, already loaded with music. Should you wish to watch a movie, we have a selection of over 700 DVDs. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel free of charge.

    Beds

    The majority of our beds measure 180 x 200 cm in size: this is 5 inches less than a King size and 11 inches larger than a Queen size. In all the rooms you will be able to choose whether to sleep in a double bed or in two single beds.

    Linens

    All our beds have a 48 cm (9 inch) thick orthopedic mattress, a feather topper and linen sheets made by "Frette" and embroidered with our hotel's logo. Should you prefer to have cotton sheets, these can be changed. There is a selection of pillows for your comfort. Nightly turndown service is provided, a special Swiss chocolate will be placed on your pillow and a pair of soft cotton slippers will be placed on your bedside mat.

    Amenities

    In the bathrooms you will find 100% cotton towels and robes by Frette and our luxurious "Eau de France" bath and body products.
