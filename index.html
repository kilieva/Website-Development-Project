    • Clair de Lune, hotel à Courchevel

    Welcome to Clair de Lune, a hotel located in the magical and festive ski resort of Courchevel. Indulge in the land of excellence and French tradition. We make every effort to satisfy your most precious desires and make sure that you have a magical experience. In Courchevel, you will find what you are looking for, an unforgettable winter holiday!

    L'Expérience

    The pursuit of excellence is at the origin of Clair de Lune. We cultivate an art of living where wellness & relaxation are the keywords. One of the secrets of Clair de Lune's success is to have elevated excellence to a way of life where authenticity rhymes above all with quality. Clair de Lune promises you an exceptional stay for an unforgettable holiday!

    Location

    Clair de Lune Hotel is located in Courchevel in the region of Rhône-Alpes, just 12.9 km from Val Thorens. It is at the heart of the French Alps. The nearest airport is Chambéry-Savoie Airport, 64.4 km from Clair de Lune Courchevel. The Jardin Alpin 1 Ski Lift is 100 meters from the hotel. Chenus Ski Lift is 200 m from Clair de Lune, while Verdons is 200 m away.
